Everything links with the same email

Whisper doesn't use separate codes for each device — Telegram and the Android app both connect using the exact same email and password you use to sign in here. Nothing to write down, nothing to lose.

1. Telegram

Tap the button below — it generates a one-time link tied to your account and opens Telegram automatically. No email or password ever appears in the link, and it expires in 10 minutes or as soon as it's used.

Once linked, alerts arrive there automatically, and you can also manage alerts directly from the telegram bot.

2. Android app

Install the app, then just log in with the same email and passwor. It runs in the background and plays a sound the moment your target price hits, even if you're busy with something else.

Download Whisper for Android (.apk)

Your phone may warn you about installing from outside the Play Store the first time — that's expected for a direct APK install; open Settings → allow installs from this source, then try again.
