Everything links with the same email

Whisper doesn't use separate codes for each device — Telegram and the Android app both connect using the exact same email and password you use to sign in here. Nothing to write down, nothing to lose.

1. Telegram

Open the Whisper bot and send it your login details to link this chat to your account:

/link your@email.com yourpassword

Open @WhisperAlertsBot on Telegram →

Once linked, alerts arrive here automatically, and you can also manage alerts directly from the chat: /setalert, /myalerts, /delete.

2. Android app

Install the app, then just log in with the same email and password — no code needed. It runs in the background and plays a sound the moment your target price hits, even if you're busy with something else.

Download Whisper for Android (.apk)

Your phone may warn you about installing from outside the Play Store the first time — that's expected for a direct APK install; open Settings → allow installs from this source, then try again.
